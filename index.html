Rebecca Jensen

UX/UI Designer

I’m a UX/UI Designer because I love asking questions and making things better. To me, being a Designer is not about having the best answer, but being committed to finding it.

My UX experience so far has been as the sole Designer on a project, affording me the opportunity to tackle the whole UX spectrum, from research and product design to detailing UI and ongoing iteration.

I am a voracious learner, both in work and hobby (just ask my board game collection). I take UX principles to heart, and have been fortunate enough to always be working on something I’m genuinely engaged with.

Am I match for your company? I’m currently looking for full-time role where I can work in a small team, with a positive work culture, and other Designers who are as excited to explore as I am.
